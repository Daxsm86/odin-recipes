Khao Soi ข้าวซอย

Description:

A coconut curry noodle soup consisting of noodles in a rich, thick coconut curry broth often with chicken or beef. A truly regional dish that varies in types of broth, noodles, protein and toppings.

Region: Northern Thailand, Laos

Origin: Southeast Asia

Recipe Credit: Ang Sarap - Contributer: Edmund



Serves 6-8

Prep: 30 minutes

Cook: 50 minutes

Total: 1 hour 20 minutes

Ingredients:

    Khao Soi Paste
  • 4 large dried red chillies, halved and seeded
  • 2 medium shallots, halved
  • 8 cloves garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro stems
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • thumb sized ginger, peeled and sliced

    • Soup
  • 500 g Chinese Egg Noodles
  • 700 g chicken thighs fillets, sliced into bite sized pieces
  • 2 400 ml cans coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • cooking oil
  • fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • salt

    • Garnishes
  • red onion, sliced
  • cilantro
  • crispy fried onions
  • chili oil
  • lime wedgs

Method:

  1. Place the chilies in a small bowl and add boiling water to rehydrate. Let steep for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain chilies reserving the liquid.
  3. Place chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, cilantro stems, coriander, turmeric, curry powder and 2 tbsp of the chili liquid in a food processor, process until it becomes a paste, add more of the soaking liquid if too thick.
  4. In a large pot add neutral cooking oil then sauté the spice paste until slightly darkened and fragrant. Be careful the spice can be hot.
  5. Add the coconut milk and chicken stock then bring it to a boil.
  6. Add chicken and simmer for 25 minutes or until chicken is tender. Set aside the chicken then using two forks shred the meat. Add chicken and the sugar and season with fish sauce. A little goes a long way!
  7. Cook egg noodles according to the packet instructions. Drain.
  8. Place the cooked noodles in bowls and ladle some of the hot soup with chicken on top. Top with the garnishes and your desired amount of chili oil.
