Khao Soi ข้าวซอย

Description:

A coconut curry noodle soup consisting of noodles in a rich, thick coconut curry broth often with chicken or beef. A truly regional dish that varies in types of broth, noodles, protein and toppings.

Region: Northern Thailand, Laos

Origin: Southeast Asia

Recipe Credit: Maangchi



Serves 4

Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 30 minutes

Total: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Kimchi

Method:

  1. Wash and cook rice.
  2. Finely cut carrots.
