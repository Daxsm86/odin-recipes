Okonomiyaki お好み焼き

Description:

A savory Japanese pancake made with a whole wheat flour batter and other ingredients. Often made with shredded cabbage, meat and seafood, topped with Okomomiyaki sauce, Japanese Mayo, Katusoboshi (Shaved Bonito flakes), Aonori and pickled ginger.

Region: Hiroshima, Osaka

Origin: Japan

Recipe Credit:The Woks of Life



Serves 4

Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 30 minutes

Total: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Kimchi

Method:

  1. Wash and cook rice.
  2. Finely cut carrots.
