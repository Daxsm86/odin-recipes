Bibimbap 비빔밥

One of the most famous Korean rice dishes. Topped with marinated meat, seasoned sautéed vegetables, kimchi, gochujang and often a fried egg.

Region: East Asia

Origin: Korea

Khao Soi ข้าวซอย

A coconut curry noodle soup consisting of noodles in a rich, thick coconut curry broth often with chicken or beef. A truly regional dish that varies in types of broth, noodles, protein and toppings.

Region: Northern Thailand, Laos, Myanmar

Origin: Southeast Asia

Okonomiyaki お好み焼き

A savory Japanese pancake made with a whole wheat flour batter and other ingredients. Often made with shredded cabbage, meat and seafood, topped with Okomomiyaki sauce, Japanese Mayo, Katusoboshi (Shaved Bonito flakes), Aonori and pickled ginger.

Region: Hiroshima, Osaka

Origin: Japan
